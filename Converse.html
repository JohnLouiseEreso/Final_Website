CONVERSE

ORIGINAL CONVERSE ALL STAR OX LOW CUT

Initially developed as a basketball shoe in the early 20th century, its design has remained largely unchanged since its introduction. The shoe consists of a stitched upper portion, and a toe cap and outsole usually made of rubber.

Features

Low-Cut Design: Offers a sleek and casual look that is versatile for various outfits and occasions.
Canvas Upper: Durable and breathable canvas material provides comfort and flexibility.
Rubber Toe Cap: Classic rubber toe cap for added durability and a signature look.
Lace-Up Closure: Traditional lace-up system for a secure and adjustable fit.
Vulcanized Rubber Sole: Offers excellent traction and flexibility.
Medial Eyelets: Provides additional airflow to keep feet cool and comfortable.
Cushioned Footbed: Ensures comfort for all-day wear.
Iconic Branding: Features the classic Converse All Star logo on the heel and tongue.
Variety of Colors: Available in a wide range of colors and patterns to suit different styles.
